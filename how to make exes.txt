Portable X - How To Make EXEs
=============================

App EXE
-------
Run:
python -m PyInstaller PortableX.spec

Installer EXE
-------------
Run:
tools\innosetup\ISCC.exe installer.iss

Outputs
-------
- dist\PortableX\PortableX.exe (plus supporting files in dist\PortableX)
- dist\Portable X Installer.exe
